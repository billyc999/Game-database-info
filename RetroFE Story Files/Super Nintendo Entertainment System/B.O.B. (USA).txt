You are a teenage ant-like robot, who is going out for a date...
However, you take your dad's car and he warns you; if you make a scratch on the car, you will never drive it again...

Of course, you make a scratch and crash on a unknown planet!
What to do now?

Guide B.O.B trough a colorful game with the strangest enemies you ever seen, to find a new car to get home with...
