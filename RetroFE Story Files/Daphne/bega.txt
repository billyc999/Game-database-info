 An animated laserdisc game where you play Bega, a cybernetic hero who must save the Earth from destruction by defeating Varge and his evil alien forces. Bega is occasionally assisted by allies Luna, Jobe and Sony at certain parts of the game.Nihon Bussan/AV Japan, the Bega's Battle maker, released 142 different machines in our database under this trade name, starting in 1978.Other machines made by Nihon Bussan/AV Japan during the time period Bega's Battle was produced include Pro Sports, Cluster Buster, Pro Tennis, Night Star, Boomer Rang'r, BurgerTime (DECO), Super Astro Fighter, Fishing, Mission-X, and Burnin' Rubber.
