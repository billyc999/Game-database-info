Heavyweight Championship Boxing is a boxing game with six athletes, all with different strengths and disadvantages. After choosing one of them, the goal is to defeat the rest of them for the title. However, the player can re-distribute the points of the athlete on the abilities speed, strength and health. During a match the goal is to bring the opponent's health down by performing moves like uppercuts or normal punches. The perspective is either first-person or third-person.
