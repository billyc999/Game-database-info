A female blue jay, named Flicky, must collect her chicks and lead them safely  back to their nest, while they are chased by two cats. The cats make her chicks wandering off and try to eat her!
Each level offers about a dozen objects to throw at and knock out the cats.
The next level starts when all chicks are returned home safely. Every level contains several objects like hamers, cups to throw at the cats and if hit knock them out.
