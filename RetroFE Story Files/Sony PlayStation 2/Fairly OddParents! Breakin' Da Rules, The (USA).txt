Da Rules book--the ultimate guide to having Fairy Godparents--has disappeared in Fairly Odd Parents: Breakin' Da Rules, and Cosmo and Wanda are left without their magical powers. Throughout this platform adventure, you play as Timmy as he enters the comic book world, battles evil viruses, avoids boy-eating plants, and finally comes to the rescue of his Fairy Godparents. Set off on an amazing adventure to recover all the pages from Da Rules book and restore his Fairy Godparents powers.
