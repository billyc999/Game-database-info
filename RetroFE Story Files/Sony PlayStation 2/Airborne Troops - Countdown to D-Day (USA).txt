You are John Welsh, 25 years old, American soldier member of the paratroopers. Two days before operation Overlord, you are sent in Normandy to secure Murat.  Airborne Troops: Countdown to D-Day is a 3D real-time shooting game, in which you will always have to choose between action and infiltration to complete your missions. You have eight weapons to choose from and the nineteen types of enemies have each their own AI.
