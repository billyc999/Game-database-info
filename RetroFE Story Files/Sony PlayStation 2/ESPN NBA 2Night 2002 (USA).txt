Another in a long line of basketball titles and a sequel to ESPN NBA 2Night. This game  features updated rosters, new character animations and the obligatory game modes: exhibition, playoffs, season, franchise and also features a soundtrack with musicians like Pete Rock and DJ Hi-Tek.  It can support up to four players and features the voice talents of ESPN Sportscenter newscaster Stuart Scott and not only is in conjunction with ESPN but also is endorsed by Tracy McGrady.
