This game is based on the hit Pixar film of the same name and same plot.  The action game sees you controlling the Incredible family members, each with their own super powers. Lift insanely heavy objects, bash through walls as Mr. Incredible, stretch to swing across chasms and grab enemies as Mrs. Incredible as well as taking control of their kids Dash (run amazingly fast) and Violet (invisibility) in the 3D action game.
