The only official licensed game of the Salt Lake 2002 Olympic Winter games.  Take part in Mens' Alpine Skiing Downhill, Ladies' Alpine Skiing Slalom, Mens' Two-man Bobsleigh, Womens' Freestyle Skiing Aerials, Mens' Ski Jumping K120 Individual and Mens' Snowboard Parallel Giant Slalom.  Multiplayer action supporting up to four players including Head-to-head split screen snowboarding.   Multiple modes of play : Olympic, Tournament, Classic and Freeform
