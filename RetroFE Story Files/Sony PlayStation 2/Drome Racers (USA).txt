Drome Racers is a futuristic racing game based on the the Lego Racers line of construction toys. Drome Racers features on- and off-road racing in vehicles that look like they were constructed out of Legos. This is an arcade-style racing game. Therefore, it features outrageous speeds and weapon powerups scattered throughout the tracks.  The main game in Drome Racers is the Career mode. In Career mode, you choose a team to join, and then compete in series of races to earn money to build betters cars. Before each series of races, you can compete in a drag race tournament to determine your starting position.  In addition to the Career mode, there are Arcade and Quick Race modes available as well.
