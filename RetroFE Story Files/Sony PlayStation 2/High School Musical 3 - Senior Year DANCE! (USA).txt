The Wildcats are back for brand new adventures in an unforgettable senior year! High School Musical 3: Senior Year for Nintendo DS invites players to an all new rhythm-based game with brand new music from the theatrical release of "High School Musical 3: Senior Year." Players can test their knowledge of "High School Musical" trivia and play as the Wildcat whose personality is most like theirs. Fans can also explore familiar locations and capture photos of "High School Musical" characters to create memorable layouts for the East High yearbook - the "High School Musical" way!
