This anthology celebrates the 10th anniversary of Neo Geo Metal Slug series and contains ports of the major past games. The included titles are:  1. Metal Slug 2. Metal Slug 2 3. Metal Slug X 4. Metal Slug 3 5. Metal Slug 4 6. Metal Slug 5 7. Metal Slug 6  There are unlockable items, bonus games, and bonus galleries containing classic poster and character art as well as other Metal Slug goodies.
