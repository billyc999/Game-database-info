Take the role of Van Helsing, legendary monster hunter, as you fight your way though Transylvania toward Count Dracula. This is a fast-paced action/adventure game where you encounter monsters like the Wolfman, the Frankenstein monster, Dr. Jekyl/Mr. Hyde, vampires, and many other terrifying creatures.  Use an arsenal of weapons and skills to destroy these monsters, including the unique "grapple gun". This game takes you to many of the places seen in the movie "Van Helsing", and features voice talent from many of the original actors including Hugh Jackman.
