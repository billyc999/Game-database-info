Worms 4: Mayhem is the third three dimensional game in the Worms franchise and brings the series back to it's roots with over-the-top action and humor. The game contains a couple of new weapons and the ability to create your own weapons by setting up parameters like the amount of clusters, the size of the blast and the method of firing the weapon. There are five different types of scenery to destroy, being Jurassic, Camelot, Arabian, Construction and Wild West.
