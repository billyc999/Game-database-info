Real Pool lets the player experience what it is like to play billiards with physics designed so that the billiards, table surfaces are cues react realistically.  There are a dozen different games to compete in including the standard 8 ball, 9 ball, rotation and different games such as Carom and Bowliards.  There are also 25 different table designs to choose from as well that are fully rendered in 3D.  It also features four different game modes including a puzzle mode and eight different AI opponents to go up against or go head-to-head against a friend.
