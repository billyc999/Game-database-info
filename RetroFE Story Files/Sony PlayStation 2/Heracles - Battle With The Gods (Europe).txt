Join the greatest of all the Greek heroes, Heracles, on an amazing adventure to rescue his best friend Pegasus from the clutches of Poseidon the evil God of the sea. Success in this fantastical quest will earn Heracles the favour of the Gods and end his banishment from Mount Olympus.  Heracles' trials continue in this epic journey across the magical lands of ancient Greece. With many foes to defeat and treacherous landscapes to navigate, Heracles will require all his superhuman strength, stamina and agility to deliver Pegasus from his evil captor.
