Zombie Virus is volume 95 in D3 Publisher's Simple 2000 series of budget games. Zombies have taken over Sunlight City. It's up to a young medical student to get behind the wheel of his ambulance and look for survivors in the dead city. Rescue engineers and mechanics so you can upgrade your ambulance with front and wheel mounted blades, rockets and brighter lights. Save police officers and soldiers to keep the morale of the survivors up or it's game over!. If Zombies climb onto your ambulance twirl the analog sticks wildly to shake them off and resume your mission.
