3D Battlemech Game. 6 scenarios with a multitude of free roaming levels. The game is mainly action orientated, allowing to customize your robot before you go to fight, or within the game after picking up various extras within the game. It has 40 different enemy types such as tanks, other mechs etc. You can destroy pretty much everything in the game. Supports 8MB memory cards and uses the vibration function of the DualShock2 controller. Single Player only.
