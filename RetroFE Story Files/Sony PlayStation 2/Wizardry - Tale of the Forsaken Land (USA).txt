What was once a peaceful kingdom of Duhan has been radically changed due to a flash that tore through the night. Thousands were killed and the kingdom is ruined, and only a handful remain after this flash devastated the land. The Queen of Duran has disappeared into the depths of the labyrinths beneath the castle. You arrive in a small town and meet several individuals looking for assistance. Several will join you on your search for treasure and the cause to this demonic uprising.
